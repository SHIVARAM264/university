ABOUT DAYANANDA SAGAR UNIVERSITY

Unveiling Legacy

Universities of great legacy across the world are the invaluable contribution of certain visionaries. Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) was founded by such a visionary — Late Sri Dayananda Sagar. His vision was to take knowledge to the people and transform today’s learners into future-ready professionals. The DSU legacy continues to grow, blending the best of tradition and modern innovation.

Best-in-Class Facilities

DSU provides exceptional infrastructure for students and faculty. From advanced laboratories to digital classrooms, from global-standard libraries to innovation centers, every facility at DSU supports holistic learning. The campus also features vibrant sports complexes and recreation areas to ensure overall development.

Inspired by Innovators

Research and innovation thrive at DSU. With over 250,000 sq. ft. of cutting-edge labs and industry tie-ups, DSU ensures that students learn beyond textbooks. Through live projects and mentorship from professionals, students gain skills aligned with real-world technological demands.

Stellar Performance – Time and Again

DSU and its parent institutions have a consistent record of academic excellence. Students regularly secure top ranks in university and national-level examinations. DSU alumni are now part of leading companies worldwide, contributing to diverse industries.

Different Courses for Different Passions

DSU offers programs across various disciplines, including Engineering, Commerce, Law, Health Sciences, and Design. Each course is designed to build strong foundations, innovation, and global readiness in students.
