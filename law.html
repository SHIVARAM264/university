The School of Law at Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) offers integrated law programs and standalone professional law degrees. The school focuses on providing strong legal foundations, courtroom exposure, practical training, and a curriculum aligned with modern legal systems and industry needs.

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum includes a combination of core law subjects, elective legal domains, and skill-based courses.

Core Legal Subjects

  • Constitutional Law
  • Civil Law
  • Criminal Law
  • Contract Law
  • Torts & Consumer Law
  • Family Law
  • Property Law
  • Administrative Law
  • Corporate & Business Law

Skill-Based & Practical Subjects

  • Moot Court & Mock Trials
  • Drafting, Pleading & Conveyancing
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)
  • Professional Ethics
  • Internship & Legal Aid Clinic work

  • Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)
  • Environmental Law
  • International Law
  • Human Rights Law
  • Banking & Insurance Law
  • Competition Law

Practical Exposure

  • Mandatory internships across courts, law firms, NGOs, corporate legal departments
  • Moot court competitions and legal debates
  • Guest lectures by judges, lawyers, and industry experts
  • Legal aid clinics for real-world case handling

Internship Opportunities

  • District and High Courts
  • Advocate Offices
  • Corporate companies (Legal departments)
  • Legal NGOs and Human Rights groups
  • Law Firms & Arbitration Centers

Placement Opportunities

  • Junior Associate roles in law firms
  • Corporate Legal Advisor roles
  • Legal Researcher
  • Litigation assistant roles under senior advocates
  • Roles in compliance, contracts, and regulatory departments

Why Choose DSU School of Law?

  • Modern law curriculum blending theory + courtroom practice
  • Strong emphasis on advocacy, communication, and legal writing
  • Industry connections for internships and practical exposure
  • Holistic development through moot courts, debates, and legal aid work
  • Experienced faculty from legal industry & academia

Conclusion

The School of Law at DSU is designed for students aiming for careers in advocacy, corporate legal roles, legal consultancy, research, judiciary preparation, or specialized legal fields such as IPR, cyber law, and international law.
