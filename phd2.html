PH.D ADMISSION

Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) offers PhD Programs in various disciplines. The University has well qualified faculty members to supervise research scholars and lead them to their Doctoral degree. These programs are available in both full time and part time modes.

  • Ph.D. in Engineering (CSE ‚Äì AI & ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, AI & Robotics; Electronics; Mechanical; Aerospace; Maths, Chemistry & Physics)
  • Ph.D. in Commerce & Management
  • Ph.D. in Basic & Applied Sciences (Life Sciences)
  • Ph.D. in Health Sciences (Pharmaceuticals, Physiotherapy, Nursing)
  • Ph.D. in Journalism & Mass Communication, Law, Computer Applications, Para Clinical Research

Admission Procedure

The number of available seats in the various specializations under different disciplines is published in the Research Supervisor's List. Candidates should discuss with a supervisor and submit the application with proper approval.

  1. Entrance Test + Interview conducted by an Expert Panel.
  2. Minimum 50% required in the Entrance Test to qualify for the interview. (Relaxation for SC/ST/OBC etc.)
  3. Selection based on a combined score (Test + Interview).

Duration of the Program

Minimum and maximum duration based on mode and prior degree:

  • Minimum 3 / 4 years for PG degree holders (Full-time / Part-time).
  • Minimum 4 / 5 years for 4-year degree holders (Full-time / Part-time).
  • One-year extension possible based on recommendation.
