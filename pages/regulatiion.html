(A) Overview / About the Ph.D. Program

Dayananda Sagar University offers a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) program across various disciplines including Engineering & Technology, Art & Design, Management, Health Sciences (Pharmacy, Dental, Nursing, Medicine), Business Administration, Paramedical Sciences, Science, Information Technology, Journalism, New Media, Arts & Humanities, Education, Law, and interdisciplinary areas. (C) Admission Process

Seats are announced online & in newspapers. Apply via DSU portal (through proper channel if employed). Applications screened by DRCC. Entrance Test + Interview conducted. 50% Research Methodology + 50% Subject-specific 50% required to qualify (5% relaxation for reserved categories). Final selection based on 70% written + 30% interview. Provisional admission upon fee payment.

(D) Categories of Ph.D. Candidates

Category Description Full-Time Full-time scholars working at DSU; may get fellowship. Project Category Those working on funded research projects; treated as full-time. Part-Time Candidates employed in recognized institutions/organizations with NOC.
