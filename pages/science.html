School of Basic & Applied Sciences â€“ Dayananda Sagar University

Overview

The School of Basic & Applied Sciences (SBAS) at Dayananda Sagar University focuses on strong foundational education in core sciences. It aims to produce graduates with analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, research orientation, and practical scientific skills that support real-world applications.

Programs Offered

  • B.Sc Programs (varies by year)
    • B.Sc in Physics
    • B.Sc in Chemistry
    • B.Sc in Mathematics
    • B.Sc in Biotechnology / Microbiology (depending on DSU availability)
    • B.Sc in Computer Science (if included in SBAS stream)
  • M.Sc Programs (specializations based on academic year)
  • Research Programs (Ph.D) in core science disciplines

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum blends theoretical foundations with laboratory work, research exposure, and application-oriented learning to prepare students for scientific, analytical, and technical careers.

Core Subjects

  • Physics (Mechanics, Optics, Electromagnetism, Quantum basics)
  • Chemistry (Organic, Inorganic, Physical Chemistry)
  • Mathematics (Algebra, Calculus, Statistics, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations)
  • Biotechnology / Biology modules (if opted)
  • Computer Science fundamentals (for CS-oriented science programs)

Laboratory & Practical Components

  • Physics labs
  • Chemistry labs
  • Biology/Biotechnology labs
  • Mathematical modeling & computational tools
  • Research-oriented experiments

Skill Development

  • Scientific reasoning & analytical skills
  • Research methodology & documentation
  • Problem-solving using mathematical and scientific models
  • Basic coding / computational simulation (for certain subjects)
  • Scientific writing & presentation

Electives (varies by specialization)

  • Data Science fundamentals
  • Environmental Science
  • Material Science
  • Bioinformatics
  • Advanced Chemistry / Physics modules

Internship & Research Opportunities

  • Internships in laboratories, research institutes, and universities
  • Industry internships (chemical labs, biotech firms, analytics companies)
  • Participation in research projects with faculty
  • Paper presentations, seminars, science exhibitions

Career & Placement Opportunities

  • Research Assistant / Lab Technician
  • Biotech / Pharma / Chemical industry roles
  • Data analysis (for math/computer science students)
  • Quality control & testing laboratories
  • Teaching and academic research roles
  • Competitive exams (CSIR NET, JAM, UPSC science roles, etc.)

Why Choose DSU School of Basic & Applied Sciences?

  • Strong academic foundation in core sciences
  • Hands-on laboratory training
  • Focus on scientific inquiry and experimentation
  • Opportunities for research, seminars, and scientific projects
  • Experienced faculty from science and research backgrounds

Conclusion

The School of Basic & Applied Sciences at DSU is ideal for students seeking to build strong scientific foundations and pursue careers in research, analytics, laboratory sciences, biotechnology, chemistry, or higher academic studies.
