About Dayananda Sagar University

Dayananda Sagar Institutions was founded in the 1960s, and Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) was established in 2014.

DSU is known for world-class interdisciplinary programs in:

  • Medicine
  • Engineering
  • Business
  • Design
  • Computing
  • Law
  • Science

DSU is proudly accredited with a NAAC A+ rating.
