DSU Research Promotion Policy

Fostering a Strong Research Culture at Dayananda Sagar University

Overview

DSU, a multidisciplinary university, promotes a vibrant research culture with the following key policies: seed grants, faculty incentives, IP protection, and more. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Key Provisions

  • Provision of a *Research Seed Grant* (ceiling: ‚Çπ 5 lakhs per proposal). :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  • Incentive for principal investigators: 8% of sanctioned external funding. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Financial support for travel (to / from conferences) and for organising conferences. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
  • DSU-KSCST IP Cell to safeguard intellectual property (patents, etc.). :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
  • Dedicated ‚ÄúResearch Professor‚Äù roles to prioritize multi- and interdisciplinary research. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Priority to PhD-qualified faculty in recruitment, or provision for staff (JRF/SRF) to pursue PhD. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
  • Support for researchers (including those without PhD) to pursue doctoral research. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
  • Creation and maintenance of high-end research facilities to support quality research. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

Seed Grant Scheme

The Seed Money Grant Scheme is a core part of the policy. Its features include:

  • Annual internal calls for seed proposals. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
  • Competitive evaluation of proposals. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
  • Periodic review of progress for awarded projects. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
  • Support for early-stage research to serve as a foundation for future external funding. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

Intellectual Property (IP) Protection

DSU, in collaboration with KSCST, runs an IP Cell that helps faculty, research scholars, and students in: :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}

  • Filing patent applications
  • Managing IP rights
  • Creating pathways to commercialize research outcomes

Promoting Interdisciplinary Research

A core committee works to: :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}

  • Encourage multi-, inter-, and trans-disciplinary research projects
  • Extend the seed grant scheme to cross-discipline proposals

Faculty Incentives & Recruitment

  • Research contributions are given due weightage in annual appraisals. :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}
  • Requirement of either a PhD or PhD registration / pursuit for entry-level faculty hiring. :contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}
  • Provision for non-PhD faculty / project staff to enrol for PhD. :contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}

DSU commits to building and maintaining advanced research labs and facilities to: :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}

  • Enable cutting-edge experimental and computational work
