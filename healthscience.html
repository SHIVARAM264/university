SCHOOL OF HEALTH SCIENCES

School of Health Sciences â€“ Dayananda Sagar University

Overview

The School of Health Sciences at Dayananda Sagar University trains students in various fields of healthcare, providing strong academic foundations, clinical skills, laboratory experience, and industry readiness. It aims to produce competent healthcare professionals through modern labs, hospital training, and expert faculty.

Programs Offered

  • B.Sc Nursing
  • BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)
  • B.Sc Allied Health Sciences (various specializations)
  • Paramedical Programs (varies by year)
  • M.Sc Programs (Allied Health Sciences / Nursing)
  • Ph.D Programs in health and life sciences

Major Specializations (Allied Health)

  • Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT)
  • Medical Imaging Technology (MIT)
  • Anaesthesia Technology
  • Emergency & Trauma Care
  • Operation Theatre Technology
  • Respiratory Therapy
  • Cardiac Care Technology

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum combines theory, practicals, and clinical training. Students gain hands-on experience in hospital settings and simulation labs.

Core Learning Areas

  • Anatomy & Physiology
  • Microbiology
  • Biochemistry
  • Pathology
  • Pharmacology
  • Nursing Foundations (for B.Sc Nursing)
  • Physiotherapy Techniques (for BPT)
  • Diagnostic & Laboratory Skills (for Allied Health)

Practical & Clinical Components

  • Clinical rotations in multispecialty hospitals
  • Ward duties and patient care
  • Diagnostic lab practice
  • Operation theatre training
  • Physical therapy labs
  • Simulation-based learning

Laboratory & Facility Highlights

  • Advanced nursing labs
  • Physiotherapy & rehabilitation labs
  • Medical imaging & radiology labs
  • Operation theatre simulation labs
  • Clinical pathology & microbiology labs
  • Attached teaching hospitals for hands-on training

Skill Development

  • Clinical decision-making
  • Emergency response skills
  • Patient communication skills
  • Diagnostic procedures
  • Therapeutic techniques
  • Teamwork & leadership in healthcare

Internship Opportunities

  • Teaching hospitals of DSU
  • Government & private hospitals
  • Diagnostic & imaging centers
  • Physiotherapy & rehabilitation centers
  • Community health centers

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse
  • Physiotherapist
  • Medical Laboratory Technologist
  • Operation Theatre Technologist
  • Cardiac / Respiratory Therapist
  • Radiology / Imaging Technologist
  • Emergency & Trauma Technician
  • Healthcare Administrator

Why Choose DSU School of Health Sciences?

  • Strong clinical exposure through DSU hospitals
  • Modern labs and updated equipment
  • Experienced faculty and medical professionals
  • Focus on real-world healthcare skills
  • Opportunities for internships, research, and industry training

Conclusion

The School of Health Sciences at DSU offers a comprehensive and practical learning environment for students aspiring to build careers in nursing, physiotherapy, laboratory sciences, emergency care, diagnostic imaging, and other healthcare professions.
