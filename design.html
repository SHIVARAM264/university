SCHOOL OF DESIGN & DIGITAL TRANSMEDIA

School of Design & Digital Transmedia â€“ Dayananda Sagar University

Overview

The School of Design & Digital Transmedia at Dayananda Sagar University focuses on modern digital media, storytelling, visual design, animation, and interactive experiences. It blends creativity with technology to prepare students for careers in digital content creation, multimedia design, and interactive media industries.

Programs Offered

  • B.Design in Digital Media / Transmedia
  • B.Design in Communication or Visual Design
  • Multimedia & Animation Certifications
  • Postgraduate Design or Media Programs (varies by year)

What is Transmedia?

Transmedia refers to storytelling across multiple digital platforms â€” combining video, graphics, web, animation, games, and interactive environments to deliver rich, engaging experiences.

Major Areas of Study

Digital Design

  • Graphic Design
  • Visual Communication
  • Typography & Layout
  • Digital Illustrations
  • Branding & Identity Design

Digital Media & Animation

  • 2D & 3D Animation
  • Motion Graphics
  • Video Editing & Production
  • Sound & Media Design
  • Visual Effects (VFX basics)

Interactive & Web Media

  • UI/UX Design
  • Interactive Storytelling
  • Web Design & Digital Interfaces
  • Game Design Basics
  • Prototyping Tools (Figma, Adobe XD)

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum combines creativity, digital tools, storytelling, and technical production. Students work on real-world projects, multimedia assignments, and interactive media designs.

Core Components

  • Design Thinking
  • Storyboarding & Narrative Development
  • Photography & Videography
  • Digital Editing Tools (Adobe Suite)
  • 3D Modeling & Animation Tools
  • Interactive Media Labs

Studio & Lab Facilities

  • Digital Media Studios
  • Animation & VFX Labs
  • Photography & Video Production Studios
  • Editing Suites
  • UI/UX & Prototyping Labs

Skill Development

  • Creativity & innovation
  • Visual storytelling

  • Interactive content development
  • Project-based portfolio building

Internship Opportunities

  • Animation & VFX studios
  • Advertising & creative agencies
  • UI/UX & digital product companies
  • Film, TV, and OTT production houses

Career Opportunities

  • Motion Graphics Artist

  • UI/UX Designer

  • Digital Content Creator
  • Visual Effects (VFX) Artist
  • Interactive Media Designer
  • Game Designer (entry-level)

Why Choose DSU School of Design & Digital Transmedia?

  • Modern studios and high-end digital media labs
  • Industry-focused curriculum
  • Creative, hands-on project-based learning
  • Faculty with industry experience in design & media
  • Strong focus on digital storytelling and interactive experiences

Conclusion

The School of Design & Digital Transmedia at DSU is ideal for students passionate about digital creativity, storytelling, multimedia production, and interactive design. It prepares students for modern creative industries through practical, technology-driven learning.
